Florida: The Sunshine State

The state of Oranges and Alligators, became the 27th state of the United States of America on March 3rd, 1845.

Fun Facts

  1. Florida is the only state that has two rivers with the same name. The Withlacoochee River flows south to north and the Withlacoochee River flows south to the Gulf.
  2. Florida is the flattest state in the United States.
  3. Florida has the longest coastline in the contiguous United States.
  4. The average Temperature in Florida ranges from 65-70 degrees Fahrenheit in the north, and from 74 to 77 degrees in the south.
